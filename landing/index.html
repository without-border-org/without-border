How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No external API keys, no SaaS subscriptions. Just your team and your server.

1

Deploy

Spin up the stack with Docker Compose on any Linux server. Gemma 4 loads locally.

2

Invite

Each team member sets their preferred language once during sign-up.

3

Chat

Write in your language. Everyone reads in theirs — automatically, in real time.

4

Automate

Enable the AI agent for async coverage and let it generate summaries on demand.
